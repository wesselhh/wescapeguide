Cape Town

The Pulse of The Mother City

Ascend the iconic heights of Table Mountain and Signal Hill for unmatched panoramic views before diving into the vibrant heart of the Mother City. Wander through the colorful streets of the Bo-Kaap, stroll the lush pathways of the Company's Garden, and uncover centuries of history at Greenmarket Square and the Castle of Good Hope.

Peninsula

Journey to the Tip of Africa

Embark on an unforgettable journey along the Cape Peninsula, carving through the dramatic cliffs of Chapman’s Peak Drive to the wild, wind-swept shores of the Cape of Good Hope. Encounter the thriving African penguin colony at Boulders Beach before immersing yourself in the historic charm and quirky seaside shops of Simon’s Town and Kalk Bay.

Winelands

Vines, Valleys & Vistas

Savour an expertly paced journey through South Africa’s premier wine valleys, showcasing their distinct terroirs, world-class architecture, and magnificent mountain vistas. From the historic, oak-lined avenues of Stellenbosch to the striking heights of Helshoogte Pass and the dramatic mountain amphitheater of Franschhoek, this day offers a visual and sensory masterclass pairing iconic estates with boutique gems.

West Coast

Wild Beauty & Coastal Charm

Journey northward along a dramatic coastline, capturing postcard-perfect views of Table Mountain from Bloubergstrand and the peaceful shores of Melkbosstrand. Venture past the striking dunes of Atlantis into the cultural heart of the region, exploring the vibrant wildflower fields, local art, and quirky heritage of Darling.

Garden Route

The Convergence Of Two Worlds

Experience the best of both worlds as you travel from the dramatic coastlines and lush, ancient canopies of the Garden Route straight into the striking, open plains of Route 62. From thrilling wildlife encounters and kayaking adventures to the dizzying heights of the world's highest bridge bungee jump and the subterranean wonders of the Cango Caves, this itinerary delivers a masterful blend of adrenaline, nature, and scenic wonder.

Overberg & Whales

Whales, Wine & Coastal Wonders

Journey into a spectacular marine-and-mountain paradise, cresting mountain passes before launching onto the deep blue waters of Walker Bay for an unforgettable whale-watching cruise. Wander along the quiet boardwalks of Stony Point to encounter a thriving African penguin colony, then swirl acclaimed cool-climate varietals in the Hemel-en-Aarde Valley before catching the golden hour on Clarens Drive.
