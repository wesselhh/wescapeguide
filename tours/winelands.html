Winelands · Full-day private tour

Vines, Valleys & Vistas

Journey down historic oak-shaded avenues, across panoramic mountain crests, and into the epicurean heart of the Cape Winelands.

Viticultural Masters

The Discerning Terroir Masterclass

This signature excursion offers an expertly paced exploration of South Africa's two premier wine valleys, designed specifically to showcase their distinct terroirs and dramatic landscapes.

Moving seamlessly from the historic oak-lined avenues of Stellenbosch to the striking, high-altitude vineyards of the Helshoogte Pass and the dramatic mountain amphitheater of Franschhoek, the day offers a visual and sensory masterclass. We pair iconic regional estates with boutique design, world-class architecture, and magnificent mountain vistas.

Highlights

Stellenbosch

Delve into South Africa’s second-oldest town and premier wine region. Begin with a morning stroll past historic, white-walled Cape Dutch architecture and under the shade of century-old oaks, before exploring the surrounding valley where rich soils and maritime breezes produce some of the country’s most celebrated, classic vintages.

Helshoogte Pass

Ascend the spectacular mountain saddle that bridges the two historic valleys. This dramatic, winding pass offers some of the most breathtaking panoramic vistas in the Cape, serving as a gateway to high-altitude vineyards where steep slopes and cool maritime breezes produce exceptionally crisp, elegant varietals.

Franschhoek

Descend into a dramatic valley steeped in French Huguenot charm. Experience a region world-renowned as a culinary capital, where a vibrant, boutique high street filled with art galleries and artisanal shops gives way to historic, high-altitude vineyards framed by towering mountain peaks.

Sample Itinerary
08:00 · Departure

Cape Town Pickup

Pickup directly from your doorstep anywhere around Cape Town.

Morning

The Birthplace of Cap Classique & Heritage Stroll

The Pioneer Spirit: We begin our morning at Simonsig Family Vineyards, a foundational pillar of South African wine heritage. Renowned for its legacy of innovation, you will sample their celebrated Kaapse Vonkel—the country's original bottle-fermented sparkling wine—alongside an array of exceptional, heritage-crafted still varietals.

Stellenbosch Cultural Walk: Next, a short drive brings us into the historic heart of Stellenbosch. Enjoy a relaxed walk on foot along its oak-shaded avenues, taking in the beautifully preserved Cape Dutch, Georgian, and Victorian architecture while soaking up the vibrant, old-world atmosphere of this historic university town.

Midday

High-Altitude Terroir & Lunch with a View

The Helshoogte Traverse: We wind our way up the spectacular Helshoogte Pass, a scenic mountain saddle offering sweeping, panoramic views of the vineyard slopes below.

Contemporary Excellence: Arrive at Tokara Wine Estate, a masterpiece of modern design seamlessly integrated into the crest of the pass. Taste signature Cabernet Sauvignon and Sauvignon Blanc, crafted from high-altitude vineyards shaped by cool maritime breezes, and take a moment to explore the estate's striking contemporary art gallery and outdoor sculpture garden.

A Relaxed Gourmet Lunch: Enjoy a vibrant, seasonal lunch at Tokara Delicatessen. Tucked away amidst olive groves, the deli features a fresh menu celebrating local ingredients, paired with expansive views across the valley floor.

Afternoon

The French Corner & Elevated Elegance

Franschhoek Village Exploration: We descend into the dramatic Franschhoek Valley, a region steeped in French Huguenot charm. Enjoy an afternoon walk down the boutique high street, wandering past independent art galleries, historic monuments, and artisanal shops with a distinctly European flair.

The Grand Finale: Conclude your viticultural journey on the historic, elevated slopes of Mont Rochelle. Nestled against the mountainside, this estate epitomizes the elegance of the Franschhoek basin. Savor a premium tasting of robust reds and crisp whites while soaking in commanding, dramatic views over the entire valley.

18:00 · Drop-off

Cape Town Return

Return transfer directly to your accommodation around 18:00.
