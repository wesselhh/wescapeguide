Cape Town · Full-Day Private Tour

The Pulse of The Mother City

From Table Mountain’s ancient peaks to historic shores and cobblestone streets—discover the soul of Cape Town.

Local Insight

Meet The Rainbow Nation

Many see the mountain, but few feel the rhythm of the city. This tour isn't just about ticking off landmarks; it’s about understanding how Cape Town’s complex history, diverse cultures, and dramatic geography weave together to create one of the most beautiful cities on Earth.

Highlights

Peaks & Panoramic Views

Beat the crowds to the summit of Table Mountain for unmatched 360-degree views, followed by dramatic coastal overlooks from Signal Hill—with an optional tandem paraglide for the ultimate adrenaline rush.

Culture, Cobbles & Local Flavour

Immerse yourself in the brilliant, candy-colored streets and rich heritage of the historic Bo-Kaap, before pausing to refuel with a delicious, authentic local lunch.

The Pulse of the Inner City

An urban exploration through Cape Town's soul. Stroll the shaded Company’s Gardens, shop Greenmarket Square, and step back in time at the historic Castle of Good Hope and Grand Parade.

Morning Pickup

Pickup directly from your doorstep anywhere around Cape Town.

Morning

Table Mountain

Beat the crowds and take the cable car to the top of the mountain for a panoramic overview of the city.

Signal Hill Lookouts

Greenmarket Square & Long Street
