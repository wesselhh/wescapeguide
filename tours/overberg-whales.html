Overberg & Whales · Full-Day Private Tour

Whales, Wine & Coastal Wonders

An incredible ocean-to-valley circuit featuring a Southern Right Charters boat cruise, Stony Point penguins, and cool-climate valley vineyards.

Marine Horizons

The Ultimate Ocean Safari Circuit

The Overberg & Whales region offers one of the most rich marine and terrestrial biodiversity showcases in South Africa. This expertly paced loop travels inland over the mountain passes in the crisp morning light, positioning you perfectly for an optimal ocean adventure before returning along spectacular coastal sea cliffs.

From tracing Southern Right whales in the deep blue waters of Walker Bay to walking among a thriving colony of African penguins nestled in wild fynbos, it's a day defined by profound natural encounters. Balanced with premium cool-climate wine tasting and a relaxed oceanfront lunch, this circuit captures the true coastal soul of the Cape.

Highlights

Southern Right Charters Cruise

Board a purpose-built catamaran from the Hermanus New Harbour for an unforgettable boat-based marine safari. Guided by expert specialists, you'll encounter Southern Right whales and rich marine life directly on the waters of Walker Bay.

Stony Point Penguin Sanctuary

Stroll along quiet, uncrowded wooden boardwalks inside an active coastal breeding sanctuary in Betty's Bay. Get uniquely close views of African Penguins, cormorants, and rock hyraxes roaming naturally through native fynbos.

Cool-Climate Wine Tasting

Ascend into the breathtaking Hemel-en-Aarde Valley to visit a pioneering boutique wine estate. Sample internationally acclaimed, terroir-driven Pinot Noir and elegant Chardonnays poured against majestic mountain backdrops.

Details

Sample Itinerary
06:30 · Departure

Early Morning N2 Outward Journey

Prompt morning pickup directly from your doorstep in Cape Town. We depart early to clear city traffic, climbing up Sir Lowry's Pass as the morning sun illuminates the vast False Bay basin below.

07:25

Peregrine Farm Stall Pit-Stop

A classic Overberg & Whales tradition. We pull into the Elgin Valley just as the doors open for a quick, premium morning break. Grab a fresh barista coffee and a selection of legendary artisanal farm bakes or traditional pies for the road.

08:30

Southern Right Charters Check-In

Arrive comfortably at the New Harbour in Hermanus for your pre-booked marine cruise. Enjoy a hot beverage at the Whale Shack during the essential safety briefing and biological overview before boarding.

09:00 · Marine Safari

Walker Bay Boat-Based Whale Watching

Launch into Walker Bay for a premier 2-hour marine cruise. Witness the raw scale and playfulness of majestic whales, fur seals, and marine birdlife from superb, unhurried vantage points on the catamaran decks.

11:15

Hermanus Village & Cliff Path Walk

Step off the boat and take a casual, scenic stroll along the famous Hermanus Cliff Paths. Explore the open markets, local art galleries, and historic seafront center of this vibrant marine town.

13:00 · Lunch Break

Coastal Culinary Stop

Sit down for a relaxed, celebratory coastal lunch tailored perfectly around fresh harbour catches, regional wines, or a lively village cafe setting overlooking the ocean waters.

14:45

Hemel-en-Aarde Boutique Wine Tasting

We leave the coast behind briefly to wind into the valley's premium boutique estates. Savor an intimate, tutored tasting of exquisite cool-climate varietals celebrated globally for their complexity and character.

16:30

Stony Point Penguins & Clarence Drive

We chart a spectacular return path via Betty's Bay to walk the quiet fynbos paths of the Stony Point penguin colony. Afterward, we catch the golden hour winding along Clarence Drive—one of the world's most dramatic coastal roads cliff-hugged against False Bay—before returning to the city.
