Peninsula · Full-Day Private Tour

Journey to the Tip of Africa

Chapman’s Peak, Cape Point, penguins, and seaside villages—one unforgettable day around the Peninsula.

Coastal Horizons

The Ultimate Marine Circuit

To truly understand the Cape, you have to follow its coastline to the very end. This journey loops the entire peninsula, contrasting the rugged, wind-carved Atlantic cliffs with the sheltered waters of False Bay.

Along the way, we navigate the engineering marvel of Chapman's Peak, visit the famous resident penguin colony in Simon's Town, and discover the distinct character of the Cape's historic seaside villages. It is a definitive, rewarding day of exploration that captures the full spirit of the coast.

Highlights

Chapman's Peak Drive

Carve through 114 curves along the sheer cliffs of Chapman's Peak Drive, where every turn reveals a more dramatic, 180-degree panoramic view of the Atlantic Ocean and the rugged Cape Peninsula.

Cape Point National Park

Journey to the southwestern tip of Africa to explore the wild, wind-swept cliffs of the Cape of Good Hope and ride the Flying Dutchman funicular up to Cape Point's iconic lighthouse for a commanding view of the colliding currents.

Coastal Towns

Stroll along the wooden boardwalks of Boulders Beach to witness a thriving colony of African penguins before exploring the quirky shops and historic charm of the Kalk Bay and Simon's Town coastal villages.

Details

Sample Itinerary
08:00 · Departure

Morning Pickup & Atlantic Seaboard

Pickup directly from your doorstep anywhere around Cape Town. We begin with a beautiful morning drive along the Atlantic Seaboard, hugging the coastline beneath the Twelve Apostles mountain range as we make our way toward the southern peninsula.

Morning

Chapman’s Peak Drive

Experience the world-renowned 114 curves of Chapman’s Peak Drive. We’ll stop at the most dramatic, elevated lookouts to capture panoramic photos of the raw Atlantic coastline at its finest.

Midday

Cape Point & Cape of Good Hope

Discover the rugged beauty of this protected coastal reserve. Take the Flying Dutchman Funicular up to the historic high lighthouse pathways, or explore the area on foot with several hiking options that showcase the raw terrain. We'll make our way down to the edge of the continent to stand at the iconic Cape of Good Hope shoreline marker.

Lunch Break

Coastal Culinary Stop

We pause for a relaxed lunch break along the False Bay coastline — tailored around fresh ocean harbor catches or a vibrant village café vibe.

Early Afternoon

Simon’s Town & Boulders Beach Penguins

Wind through a historic naval outpost on our way to the wooden boardwalks of Boulders Beach, getting up-close views of the famous resident African penguin colony.

Late Afternoon

Kalk Bay, Muizenberg & Drop-off

Drive through the peninsula's iconic coastal outposts, soaking in the bohemian energy of Kalk Bay and the spirited surf vibe of Muizenberg. This wraps up our loop of the coast before heading back to your accommodation.
